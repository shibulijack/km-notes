Smart Leadership Requirements

The knowledge chain represents a series of steps which determines the potential of a learning organization. One approach involves the following steps:

  • Assessment of the core competency of the organization.

  • Response to organization's shortcomings (internal).

  • Excellent knowledge of the external market and the nature of competition in the market.

  • Online response to company's external environment.

  • Measuring the return on time.
