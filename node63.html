Integration Alternatives:

  • Technical Integration: Occurs through the organization's LAN environment, the resident mainframe, or existing IS infrastructure.

  • Knowledge Sharing Integration: Often requires the upgradation of the LAN, the mainframe, or lines.

  • Decision Making Flow Integration: Suggests that the way the KM system assesses a problem situation should match the user's way of thinking.

  • Workflow Reengineering: Considered when implementation of the new KM system can propose changes in the workplace.
