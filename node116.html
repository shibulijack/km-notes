Portals and Business Transformation

  • Usually problems arise from the following two fundamental aspects underlying the present computing technology:

    • The explosion in the quantity of business information already captured in electronic documents leads many organizations to lose their grip on the information as they upgrade their processes and transform to new systems.

    • The fast speed with which the quantity (and kinds) of information content is growing, indicates that what is needed to meet the challenges is a strict internal discipline which can help to expose and integrate the sources of enterprise knowledge.

  • Types of pressures faced by most organizations:

    • Shorter time to market

    • More demanding investors/customers

    • Knowledge worker turnover
