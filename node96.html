Intranet

  • Serves the internal needs of an organization.

  • Links knowledge workers and managers around the clock and automates intraorganizational traffic.

  • An organization needs intranet if:

    • A large pool of information is to be shared among large number of employees.

    • Knowledge transfer needs to be done in hurry.
