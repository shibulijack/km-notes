Knowing how the Organization handles Mistakes

  • Mistakes are bound to be made and there can be cost associated with it.

  • Tolerance for mistakes usually allows room for learning to take place.

  • Some of the best learnings in business can take place by the method of trial and error.

  • Organizations that are successful in turning knowledge into action are usually found to inspire respect and admiration, rather than fear or intimidation.
